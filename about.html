About Linked By Books

Linked By Books is more than just a book club. We are a welcoming community of readers who believe that books have the power to inspire, educate, and bring people together. Every month we meet to share ideas, learn from different perspectives, and enjoy meaningful conversations.

🌸 Our Mission

To create a welcoming space where readers connect, think deeply, and grow through books and conversations.

💜 Our Vision

To build a vibrant community of passionate readers who inspire one another through stories, thoughtful discussions, and lifelong learning.

✨ Our Values

📚 Love for Reading
🤝 Respect for Different Opinions
💬 Meaningful Conversations
🌱 Personal Growth
💖 Kindness & Inclusion
📖 Lifelong Learning
